The Factor-Augmented Nelson-Siegel Model with Backward-Looking Macroeconomics Variables and Time-Varying Parameters: Forecasting the Yield Curve in the US

This paper proposes forecasting models that combine factor augmented dynamic Nelson-Siegel (FADNS) and DNS-Svensson models with time-varying parameters to predict the US yield curves. The results suggest that the forecasting models significantly improve the predictive accuracy of extant models in the literature.
